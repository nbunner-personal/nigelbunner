--- layout: glossary title: What it a standards compliant web site? permalink: /glossary/standards-compliant.html ---

All the web sites I create are coded with consideration made of the HTML guidelines recommended by The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). By following these guidelines and making their code Standards Complient Web Designers can help ensure their designs look and work the same across the wide range of Web Browsers available, including Screen Readers and Browsers used by the Blind and Visually Impaired.
